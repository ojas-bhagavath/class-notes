            Course & MSM423 Differential Geometry\\\hline

            Learning Outcomes & \begin{itemize}
                \vspace{3mm}
                \item Understanding the classical interpretation of various curvatures of a surface and their relation to geodesics.
                \item Understanding the local and global geometry of smooth manifolds and smooth vector bundles.
            \end{itemize}\\\hline
                Syllabus & \begin{itemize}
                    \vspace{3mm}
                    \item Gauss curvature, Gauss curvature formula in terms of first and second fundamental  forms. Intrinsic property of the Gauss curvature. (6)
                    \item Covariant derivative of a vector field along a curve; Relation between covariant  derivative and total curvature of a curve; A geodesic as a curve with vanishing covariant  derivative. (6)
                    \item Manifolds: Definition, examples, Tangent vector space at a point, Basis of the tangent  vector space. Smooth functions on a manifold, maps between Manifolds. Differential of  a map. (6)
                    \item Sub-manifolds; Regular value theorem. Lie groups, examples; Submersion, Immersion  and Embeddings. (6)
                    \item Smooth vector bundles, smooth sections, Dual bundles, existence of local sections. (5)
                    \item Tangent bundles; Smooth vector fields; Lie bracket of smooth vector fields; Co-tangent  bundles; Differential 1-forms. (5)
                    \item Differential p-forms. Orientation. Exterior derivative. Closed and exact forms. Integration  of a p-form on a p-dim sub manifold. Stokes theorem. (6)
            \end{itemize}\\\hline
